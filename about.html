I haven't updated this blog for a long time, especially since joining TikTok. After graduating, my first job was as a Data Scientist at Walmart, where I moved up from an entry-level position to a Senior Data Scientist in a year and a half. Later, I joined TikTok to work on video recommendation algorithms. I'll soon be joining Snap to work on Ads Ranking. During my time at TikTok, work has been extremely busy but has also provided significant growth opportunities.

In my free time, I enjoy playing tennis and scuba diving. I am a PADI certified scuba diver. I also occasionally play PS5 games; recently, I've returned to Cyberpunk 2077 and have been immersed in Night City.
