Jiapei Zhuang belief in Reductionism since high school. Reductionism and Feynman are two of main motives that have led him to the temple of Physics. Motives are also negative one, which are the escaping from noisy of everyday life, endless shifting desires and loneliness.

The belief crisis happened in the first month of his undergraduate when emergent phenomena was explained by the paper《More Is Different》, and more discusion in Book《Complexity: A Guided Tour》. The outcome of this crisis was the coexist of Emergentism and Reductionism, a contradiction terms, in mind.

After attempts at Laser crystal growth; Dynamic in complex Network; APREES measurement of the microscope of the electrons behave. Jiapei decide to engage in theoretical physics, to construct mathematical models of world, and then test the predictions of such models against observation and experiments. This is the way physicists Dialogue with Nature, to understand how laws unfolded themselves in the objects of the nature world.
